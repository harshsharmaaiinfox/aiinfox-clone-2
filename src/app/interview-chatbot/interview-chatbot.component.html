WHAT IS AN INTERVIEW CHATBOT?

An Interview Chatbot is an AI-driven tool designed to conduct preliminary interviews with job candidates. It asks relevant questions, records responses, and assesses candidates based on predefined criteria. This project focuses on creating a chatbot capable of conducting technical interviews by analyzing a candidate's resume and job description to tailor interview questions specific to the job role and the candidate's qualifications.

Key Features

Extract Skills
Analyzes and extracts skills from both the candidate's resume and the job descriptions.
Resume Summarization
Provides a concise summary of the candidate's resume, highlighting key points.
Generate Technical interview Question
Creates interview questions tailored to matched skills considering the candidate's experience level.
Speech-To-Speech Intervies
Conducts the interview via voice, simulating a real-life interview experience.
Interview Summary And History
Summarizes the interview session and saves the history of questions and responses for future reference.

Project Overview

The Interview Chatbot project addresses the inefficiency of manual candidate screening in the recruitment process, where large volumes of applicants make it difficult to evaluate consistently and quickly.

Problem Statment

The project aims to solve the issues of time-consuming and biased candidate screening by automating the initial stages of interviews, ensuring a more efficient and objective process.

Evidence Of The Problem

The need for faster, more consistent hiring processes is widely recognized in competitive job markets, where delays and biases in recruitment can lead to missed opportunities and inefficiencies.

Functional Tabs

The functional tabs provide key features for managing job descriptions, candidates, interview questions, and scheduling meetings efficiently.

Job Description
Add job descriptions and related details.
Add Candidate
Add candidates along with their resumes. Automatically generate questions for the candidate.
Questions
Edit, delete, or add questions for specific candidates.
Schedule Meeting
Schedule interviews. The system saves interview history, audio files, and summaries post-interview.

Technologies Used

This system leverages cutting-edge technologies, frameworks, and tools to ensure seamless functionality, high performance, and scalability. It integrates AI-powered models, backend frameworks, and communication APIs to provide an efficient and intelligent solution.

Interview Chatbot Features

The Interview Chatbot is equipped with advanced capabilities to streamline the hiring process. It analyzes resumes, generates customized questions, adapts to candidate responses, manages interactions, provides feedback, and integrates seamlessly with HR systems to enhance recruitment efficiency.
