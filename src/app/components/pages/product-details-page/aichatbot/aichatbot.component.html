Classroom Chatbot

A classroom AI chatbot is a smart tool made for schools. It works on computers or phones and helps students learn better. It fits into digital learning tools or works alone, giving students interactive help and support as they learn.

Using advanced language skills, it gives personalized help, quick feedback, and adjusts how it teaches based on how students are doing. This makes learning more interesting and helps students do better in school.

It simplifies the interview preparation process, ensures that interviews are focused and relevant, and helps hiring managers assess candidates more effectively and efficiently.
