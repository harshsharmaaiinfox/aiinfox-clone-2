Process

Our Web Design Process

Discovery

This is our very first step to knowing all about your business, the target audience you are trying to connect with, and lastly your business goals to understand the requirements of the project.

Design

Web designers of AiInfox, strictly follow the approach where wireframes and mock-ups are prepared based on the inputs provided by you. We make corrections till we perfectly capture your vision.

Development

After the final approval for the designing part, our web development expert team comes into play. The team makes use of the latest technologies and brings the design to life.

Testing

Once the development part is complete, we conduct in-depth testing where we ensure the website is fully functional, optimized, and error-free for all browsers and devices.

Launch

The final step is to launch the website, once it is approved from our client's side. We keep providing ongoing support to make it a success with the targeted audience in the most efficient way.
